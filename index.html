01

THE PROBLEM

Consulting firms run on knowledge trapped in a few people's heads. Proposals get rebuilt from scratch every time, contracts and client records drift out of sync, and the real bottleneck is rarely the writing. It is waiting on an architect or developer to stop building long enough to scope the work, price it, and estimate the hours. Every hour spent on that is an hour not spent in front of a client.

02

WHAT I BUILT

I chose Claude and Notion as the foundation, fit them alongside the tools the team already relied on, then trained the system on every scope of work we had ever produced. It learned what we build, what we charge, and how long the work actually takes. On top of that sits a library of purpose-built skills, each trained on our own document standards, so the system can scope, price, and draft an engagement on its own, in our format and our voice.
